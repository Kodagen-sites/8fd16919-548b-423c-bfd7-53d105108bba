Cinematic golden-hour interior of a warm artisan bakery in Lagos, Nigeria. A rustic reclaimed-wood counter dusted with flour, a row of freshly baked round sourdough loaves with deeply scored, burnished amber-brown crusts cooling on a woven rattan tray. Soft steam rising gently. Warm morning sunlight streams in low from the left through a large window, casting long honeyed light and soft shadows across cream-colored walls. Shallow depth of field, creamy bokeh, terracotta and amber tones, a few sprigs of wheat and a folded linen cloth. Photorealistic, 35mm film look, warm color grade, cozy and inviting, high detail, no text, no people.
